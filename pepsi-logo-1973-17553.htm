Composition

The sticker consists of three components::

  1. Support or siliconed paper
  2. Vinyl adhesive
  3. Mask or band

The carrier is used to apply the adhesive to the desired surface.

Final result after mounting

These adhesives don't have a specific base (in other words, the surface where the adhesive is applied acts as the base). This material is similar to the one used on commercial vans. The surface can resist pressure washing 24 hours after installation.
